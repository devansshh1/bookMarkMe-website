How it works

Four steps. Zero friction.

1

Open ChatGPT

Start your conversation exactly as you normally would.

2

Open BookMarkMe

Launch the sidebar from the extension and keep it open while you work.

3

Save the important part

Click the bookmark control next to any message you want to come back to.

4

Jump back instantly

Select the bookmark from your list and return to that exact point in the conversation.

Privacy & Security

Your chats stay yours.

BookMarkMe is designed to be useful without becoming another tool that wants to inspect or store your work.

Stored locally

All bookmarks are saved directly on your device using Chrome storage.

No data collection

We never see, transmit, or store your chat content.

Minimal permissions

Access is limited to `chatgpt.com` so navigation features work where you actually need them.
